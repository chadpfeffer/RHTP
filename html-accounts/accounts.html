Reference architecture template priority

These templates are the starting structure for every brief. Priority order is based on the NOFO category combinations that appear across the 27 Engage Now providers — your team should build these in order.

#1 Rural Care Backbone + Workforce (G+E) Template — Your team to build

SD-WAN spine connecting rural sites → Webex Meetings/Endpoints for specialist consults & CME → Meraki for clinical site connectivity → Secure Access for clinician BYOD.
Universal — applies to nearly all 27 accounts. Build this one first.

#2 Population Health Analytics (A) Template — Your team to build

Splunk ingestion of claims + EHR + SDOH data → dashboards for chronic disease cohorts → Webex Contact Center AI for proactive outreach.
Applies to 20 of 27 accounts (Prevention is the 2nd most common category).

#3 Clinical AI at the Edge (I) Template — Your team to build

UCS/Unified Edge at rural hospitals → local model inference (ambient docs, imaging triage) → SD-WAN back to central data plane.
Applies to 4 of 27 accounts but highest differentiation vs competitors.

#4 Tele-Behavioral Health (H) Template — Your team to build

Webex Instant Connect for on-demand telepsych → Contact Center for crisis line → Meraki at clinic / ED / school sites.
Rare in Engage Now providers but 15-20% budget category — high leverage when present.

#5 Digital Front Door (C) Template — Your team to build

Webex Contact Center + AI Agents → patient self-scheduling, triage, navigation → ThousandEyes monitoring the patient experience.
Applies to 3 of 27 accounts.

#6 Security + Cisco Capital (cross-cutting) Template — Your team to build

XDR, ISE, Firewall, Secure Access protecting the entire RHTP investment → Cisco Capital operating lease to convert CapEx into allowable OpEx under 2 CFR 200.465.
Appears in every brief — wraps all the above.
