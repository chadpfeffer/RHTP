1 Growing Care Coordination — Medical Operations Coordination Center (MOCC)
24/7 statewide hub coordinating patient transfers, EMS resources, hospital capacity for trauma, stroke, psychiatric, and maternal care. Monitors capacity and enables surge response. 2 Growing Community Connections through Indiana 211
Statewide 211 infrastructure for health/human services resource data with closed-loop referral and outcome tracking. EMR and non-EMR integration across providers.		 TBD B SUBCONTRACTOR PLAY HIE integration, API development, interoperability solutions for 211 platform ACTIVE — Year 1 vendor selection and integration planning
3 Improving Patient Outcomes through Enhanced Interoperability and Tech
Connect 450+ facilities to Indiana Health Information Exchange (IHIE) for secure data sharing. Integrate EMS into HIE and care coordination via Mobile Integrated Health (MIH).		 TBD B SUBCONTRACTOR PLAY HIE connectivity, EMS data integration, consent management for behavioral health ACTIVE — Year 1 readiness assessment and pilot implementation
4 Pediatric and Obstetric Readiness in Rural Emergency Departments
Partnerships with IU School of Medicine to conduct readiness assessments, provide training/equipment, and establish standards for rural ED pediatric/OB care.		 TBD AC SELL TO PROVIDER Training management systems, equipment tracking, clinical workflow optimization ACTIVE — Year 1 grant contracts to IU, MOU with IDHS, readiness standards development
5 Cardiometabolic Standards of Care in Rural Indiana
Eight Centers of Excellence with holistic, multidisciplinary cardiometabolic care. Nutrition education, lifestyle medicine training (700 certifications Y1), and Food is Medicine feasibility study.		 TBD A SELL TO PROVIDER Data analytics for chronic disease management, wearable health tech integration ACTIVE — Y1: site identification, vendor partnerships (American Heart Assoc, YMCA Alliance)
6 Access to Hospital Post-Discharge Medications (Meds-To-Beds)
Ensure rural hospital patients receive prescribed medications at discharge, at outpatient prices. Clinical staff review medications and support transition of care.		 TBD A SUBCONTRACTOR PLAY Pharmacy systems integration, discharge medication workflow automation ACTIVE — Y1: hospital readiness assessment, working group convening, hospital RFFs
7 Expanding Teleconsultation Capabilities
Expand telehealth and teleconsultation to improve access to specialists. Comprehensive landscape assessment and pilot program identification for high-impact conditions.		 TBD B PRIME ELIGIBLE Telehealth platform and infrastructure, specialty consultation integration ACTIVE — Y1: landscape assessment and opportunity evaluation
8 Expanding Telehealth Access and Infrastructure
Strengthen telehealth infrastructure across regions and specialties. Infrastructure investment to close gaps in access, specialty availability, and underserved populations.		 TBD B PRIME ELIGIBLE Broadband/connectivity upgrades, telehealth platform standardization ACTIVE — Y1: landscape assessment informing regional GROW activities
9 Growing our Rural Health Paraprofessional Workforce
Establish career pipelines for health, behavioral, and oral health paraprofessional roles. Rural healthcare academies, CHW strategic plan, employer TA playbook, and retention innovation.		 TBD C SELL TO PROVIDER Workforce management systems, training platform development ACTIVE — Y1: CHW strategic plan, employer TA playbook, RFA development for academies
10 Clinical Training and Readiness
Expand physician training pathways and increase clinical rotation capacity in rural settings. Focus on family med, internal med, peds, OB/GYN, psychiatry. Preceptor incentives up to $10k/year.		 TBD C SELL TO PROVIDER Training management, provider incentive tracking, residency rotation scheduling ACTIVE — Y1: GME strategic plan, physician incentives (summer 2026), preceptor registry (2027)
11 Growing our Rural Behavioral Health Workforce
Grow and retain rural behavioral health workforce through career pathways, peer support training, threat assessment training, clinical social worker recruitment.		 TBD C SELL TO PROVIDER Behavioral health workforce management, training coordination systems ACTIVE — Y1: RFFs for clinical social workers and retention, training grants (May 2026 release)
