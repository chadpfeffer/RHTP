VENDOR MODEL — State issues RFPs; vendors apply, not providers
Procurement model: Four competitive initiatives managed by NVHA with State Purchasing Division: (1) Rural Health Outcomes Accelerator Program (15% funds, RFP); (2) Nevada Rural Health System Flex Fund (20%, RFA); (3) Workforce Recruitment & Rural Access Program (40%, RFP/RFA); (4) Rural Health Innovation & Technology (15%, RFP). Competitive procurement with registered vendor requirement.
